The Culture

The Culture is UMTV’s first and only show highlighting the Black experience. This 15-20 minute program covers cultural, political, entertainment, and sports. Stories are produced and edited by students at University of Miami that identify with "The Culture". Join us as we dive into understanding each other and the world we live in. I act as associate producer of the show, helping with the filming and editing of content as well as develop ideas for our packages.

Episode 1

On this premier episode of the Culture we debue our segments the Round Table, Hella Shade, and Athletic Aesthetic.
