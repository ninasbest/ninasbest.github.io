Nina Best is currently a student at the University of Miami where she is pursuing a career in sports communications.

In an effort to pursue this career, she has volunteered to work on the UMTV show SportsDesk and work with the UM athletic teams as a video production assitant.

Her dedication to excellence, tenacity and commitment will be an asset in her pursuit of her goals.

She is enthusiastic about the area of sports, she knows that this is a tough market for women but believes that with determination and a great work ethic she will be successful.

She has spent several years as a swim instructor, which she feels has made an impact on the many swimmers that she has taught. This was also a period of growth for her in building on leadership and confidence.

In addition, she spends many volunteer hours supporting United Black Students, Best Buddies and A Week for Life.
